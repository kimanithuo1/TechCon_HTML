History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2020 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts trying to cope with loneliness during covid19 period. Over the last 3 years, it has grown into one of the most promising tech conferences in the Africa, attracting industry leaders, startups, and innovators from around the continent.

Our Mission

Our mission is to share knowledge , inspire innovation, and offer a platform for discussing the latest trends and tech predictions. TechCon is committed to promoting how tech knowledge is accessed

Notable Past Speakers

Strive Masiyiwa

Strive Masiyiwa, founder of Econet Zimbambwe, shared his insights during TechCon 2022. Econet Wireless Zimbabwe Limited is the largest mobile telecommunications, technology and digital solutions company in Zimbabwe. Over the past 20 years Econet which is by far the market leader in its industry with over 12.4 million connected customers, has invested millions of US dollars in deploying the widest and most robust 2G, 3G and 4G networks in the country.

Mitchell Elegbe

Interswitch Founder Mitchell Elegbegave an inspiring talk on the future of fintech and its impact on everyday life at TechCon 2021. Mitchell Elegbe is the founder of Interswitch Group, a pioneer in Nigerian fintech. The company introduced innovative payment processing solutions and financial services, transforming the way transactions are conducted in Africa.
